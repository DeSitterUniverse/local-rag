# Stress Advice

Stress is a common biological reaction. Managing stress levels is important for long term health. Focus on breathing, taking breaks, and maintaining positive relationships.

## Immediate Relief
When you feel overwhelmed, try the 4-7-8 breathing technique. Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 7 seconds, exhale for 8 seconds. This resets the nervous system.

## Long Term Strategy 
Regular exercise, good sleep hygiene, and minimizing caffeine can drastically lower baseline anxiety over time.
